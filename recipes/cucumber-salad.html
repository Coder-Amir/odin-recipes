CucumberSalad

Descriptioin

A cucumber salad is a fresh and light dish featuring thin slices of cucumber. Tossed with red onions, fragrant dill, and a zesty dressing of vinegar, olive oil, and seasonings, it's a crisp and invigorating treat. The combination of textures and flavors makes it a perfect side dish for any meal, bringing a burst of refreshment to your plate.

Ingredients

  • Cucumbers: 2-3 medium-sized cucumbers, preferably English cucumbers or other varieties with thin skins.
  • Red Onion: 1 small red onion, thinly sliced (optional).
  • Fresh Dill: A handful of fresh dill leaves, chopped.
  • White Vinegar or Rice Vinegar: About 2-3 tablespoons.
  • Olive Oil: About 2 tablespoons.
  • Sugar: 1-2 teaspoons, to taste.
  • Salt: To taste.
  • Black Pepper: To taste.

Steps

  1. Prep Cucumbers: Wash and slice 2-3 medium cucumbers into thin rounds.
  2. Slice Onion: Thinly slice a small red onion.
  3. Make Dressing: Mix 2-3 tbsp vinegar, 2 tbsp olive oil, 1-2 tsp sugar, salt, and black pepper in a bowl.
  4. Combine Ingredients: Add cucumbers and sliced onion to the bowl.
  5. Add Fresh Dill: Chop and add a handful of fresh dill leaves.
  6. Toss Gently: Toss everything gently to coat with dressing.
  7. Chill: Let the salad sit in the fridge for 15-30 minutes to meld flavors.
  8. Adjust Seasoning: Taste and adjust salt, pepper, or other flavors if needed.
  9. Serve: Enjoy the refreshing cucumber salad chilled.
