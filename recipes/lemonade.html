Lemonade

Description

Lemonade is a refreshing and tangy citrus beverage enjoyed worldwide for its delightful balance of sweet and tart flavors. Made by combining freshly squeezed lemon juice, water, and sugar, it offers a thirst-quenching experience, especially on hot days. With its vibrant yellow hue and zesty aroma, lemonade embodies a sense of rejuvenation and relaxation. Whether served over ice, infused with herbs or fruits, or enjoyed as a classic base for creative mixology, lemonade remains a timeless and universally cherished drink, perfect for brightening up any occasion.

Ingredients

  • Freshly squeezed lemon juice (from about 4-6 lemons)
  • Water (about 4 cups)
  • Granulated sugar (to taste, usually 1/2 to 1 cup)
  • Ice cubes
  • Lemon slices (for garnish)
  • Optional: Mint leaves or other herbs for added flavor

Steps

  1. Squeeze the Lemons
  2. Prepare Simple Syrup (Optional)
  3. Mix Lemon Juice and Water
  4. Sweeten the Lemonade
  5. Chill and Serve
